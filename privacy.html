Creator OS · Social Tweebs

Privacy Policy

Effective 18 June 2026

Creator OS is a tool for content creators, operated by Social Tweebs ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data we collect when a creator connects their Instagram account, how we use it, how long we keep it, and how a creator can delete it. We do not sell personal data.

In short: If you connect your Instagram, we read only your own profile and analytics, and we show them back to you inside Creator OS so you can understand your audience and find brands that fit. We never post for you, never message anyone on your behalf, and you can disconnect and erase your data at any time.

1. Who we are

Social Tweebs operates Creator OS at app.socialtweebs.ai. For any privacy question or request, contact privacy@socialtweebs.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Account details you give us: your name, Instagram handle, email address and a password you choose when you create an account.
  • Instagram data, only if you connect your account (via Instagram Login, using the permissions instagram_business_basic and instagram_business_manage_insights):
    • Your profile: username, account type, follower count, media count and profile picture.
    • Your media: your recent posts and their basic engagement (likes, comments, reach).
    • Your insights: reach, content views, profile visits, engagement, and the aggregate age, gender and location breakdown of your audience. Audience demographics are aggregate counts only; we never receive the identity of your individual followers.

3. How we use it

  • To show you a private analytics dashboard of your own account inside Creator OS.
  • To match you with brands whose target audience fits your real audience, and to help you prepare pitches with genuine numbers.
  • To confirm that the connected Instagram account belongs to you.

We use your Instagram data only to provide these features to you. We do not use it for advertising, we do not build profiles of your followers, and we do not post or send messages on your behalf.

4. How we store and protect it

Your data is stored on our managed database (Supabase), encrypted at rest. Your Instagram access token is kept in a restricted, internal-only store and is never exposed to your browser or to other users. Access is limited to the systems that run Creator OS.

5. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data and we do not share your Instagram data or audience breakdown with third parties for their own use. We rely on infrastructure providers (such as our hosting and database provider) strictly to operate the product. We may disclose data if required by law.

6. Data retention & deletion

You can remove your Instagram data at any time, in any of these ways:

  • In the app: open Creator OS → Insights → Manage data · disconnect. This deletes your stored access token and insights immediately.
  • From Instagram: remove Creator OS under Instagram → Settings → Apps and websites. Instagram notifies us and we delete your data automatically.
  • By request: email privacy@socialtweebs.com and we will erase your data within 30 days.

Our data deletion endpoint is available at this link. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data and any connected Instagram data.

7. Your rights

You can access, correct or delete your personal data, and withdraw your consent to the Instagram connection at any time, by using the in-app controls above or by emailing us. We honour applicable data-protection rights, including under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act.

8. Children

Creator OS is for professional creators and is not directed at anyone under 18.

9. Changes

If we change this policy we will update the date above and, for material changes, notify you in the app.
